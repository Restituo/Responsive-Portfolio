About Me

My name is Richard Chen. I have always been interested in technology. From a young age I started using our family computer and I always pondered about how web pages are created. As I got older, my love for technology blossomed when I found out about video games. My first console was a Playstation, I moved to a Playstation 2, and so forth as new consoles are being developed often.

Before finishing collage, I began to research what kind of career I could eventually acquire in technology. Web development sparked my interest and led me to where I am today. I know that this may be a difficult journey to eventually make my dream a reality; however, I know I will not give up because I am a hard worker who enjoys challenges and will do everything in my power to succeed.
